INTERVIEW EXPERIENCE – TCS ON-CAMPUS PLACEMENT

This document contains a real on-campus interview experience for Tata Consultancy Services (TCS), structured for Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems. The experience is organized round-wise with focus areas, evaluation criteria, and candidate observations.

==================================================================

COMPANY: :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0} (TCS)
Interview Type: On-Campus Placement
Final Outcome: Selected (Offer Letter Received)

==================================================================

ROUND 1: Aptitude Test

Evaluation Focus:
- Quantitative aptitude
- Logical reasoning
- Accuracy over number of attempts

Candidate Details:
- Attempted approximately 23 questions
- Candidates with similar attempts cleared the round
- Accuracy was more important than attempting all questions

Key Insight:
- Higher attempts do not necessarily lead to higher scores
- Strong understanding of basic quantitative concepts is critical

==================================================================

ROUND 2: Technical Interview

Duration:
- Approximately 25 minutes

Topics Covered:
- Self-introduction (education, family, achievements)
- Core subjects:
  - Computer Networks
  - Operating Systems
  - Data Structures and Algorithms
  - DBMS
  - Java, C, C++
- Academic project discussion:
  - Project explanation
  - Reason for chosen approach
  - Alternative methods
- OSI model basics
- DBMS normalization and differences between normal forms
- Data structures fundamentals
- Inheritance concepts in OOPS

Evaluation Style:
- Basic questions asked in depth
- Follow-up questions used to test conceptual clarity

Candidate Observation:
- Strong fundamentals were more important than advanced knowledge

==================================================================

ROUND 3: Management Interview

Interviewer Profile:
- Senior HR professional with approximately 15 years of experience

Evaluation Focus:
- Confidence under stress
- Career goals and motivation
- Decision-making ability
- Organizational fit

Sample Questions:
- Why TCS?
- Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
- Monthly expenses
- Expectations from TCS
- College attendance and learning habits
- Approach to learning unfamiliar technologies
- Handling tedious or undesired tasks
- Location flexibility

Candidate Observation:
- Interviewer deliberately tested confidence through neutral or negative reactions
- Remaining calm and confident was critical

==================================================================

ROUND 4: HR Interview

Evaluation Focus:
- Personal background
- Self-awareness
- Motivation and clarity

Sample Questions:
- Meaning of candidate’s name
- Strengths and weaknesses (with examples)
- Why TCS? (revisited)
- Questions from the candidate to the interviewer

Candidate Observation:
- Short and conversational
- Easier compared to management interview

==================================================================

FINAL RESULT

- Offer letter announced at midnight
- Positive emotional outcome for candidate and family

==================================================================

End of TCS Interview Experience Document
